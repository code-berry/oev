Beautiful Buildings Awards

Urban Regeneration takes place one building at a time. Beautiful Building Awards acknowledge and thank those property or business owners who embrace their role in the revitalization by investing in the beautification of their buildings. Those who have received this award have demonstrated their neighbourhood commitment as well as setting a great example for others to do the same.
