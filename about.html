About Us

Our Mandate: Create a vibrant, diverse, and sustainable commercial corridor, at the heart of an inclusive community, where more people live, work, shop, play and produce.

The Old East Village BIA is proud to represent the large variety of businesses and property owners that is the Old East Village Commercial Corridor. We work with small and large scale developments as well as a variety of business models ranging from sole proprietorships, corporations to worker owned co-operatives. We encourage all current and potential Old East Village Businesses to contact us at your convenience to further discuss BIA assistance.
