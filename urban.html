Urban Village Design Manual

The Old East Village Urban Design Manual was created in partnership with the City of London Urban Design Planners to create guidelines for façade restoration and development. The ongoing revitalization of the Old East Village relies on quality façade renovation to provide an experience through urban design that is unlike anywhere else in London. The OEV Design Manual strives to motivate property owners to conform to or complement the original heritage aspects of the area built form in order to continue the trend of beautiful buildings in the Old East Village.
